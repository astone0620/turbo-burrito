Rainbow Six Siege

What it is

Rainbow Six Siege is a tactical first person shooter made by Ubisoft. The main selling point of Rainbow Six Siege are the operators. Operators have special abilities, such as but not limited to,

  • A tacticool crossbow
  • A heartbeat sensor
  • A one way balistic mirror
  • And a shock drone
